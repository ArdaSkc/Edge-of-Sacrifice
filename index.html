Edge of Sacrifice

In a dystopian future, Violette, a young princess, escapes the destruction of her kingdom with a talisman of immense power, the key to activating a world of dormant robots. Fleeing by sea, she washes ashore on a desolate beach, pursued by enemy soldiers. Surrounded, Violette must choose between her own survival or sacrificing her life to unleash the army and save her world.
